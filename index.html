While I may not pursue a career in the chemical industry, this experience has fundamentally shaped how I approach problem-solving, data analysis, and scientific inquiry. This portfolio tells the story of my growth: from finding my footing in a manufacturing environment, to conducting data analysis and research using advanced instruments, to applying critical thinking in ways that pushed me well beyond my comfort zone.
